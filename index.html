What the engine actually does

01

Color science with intent

The engine cools the white balance, pushes shadows toward cyan, remaps blues toward teal, bends greens toward olive-yellow, and keeps red/orange objects unnaturally alive.

02

Tone curve before texture

A hard black/white point remap and steep S-curve create dense silhouettes before the synthetic sensor field is injected back into the image.

03

Digital damage, not nostalgia

Low internal resolution, unsharp halos, RGB/luma speckle, Bayer dither, bloom restraint, and JPEG quantization make the output feel like an app-specific camera pipeline rather than film.
